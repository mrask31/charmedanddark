# Smutty Good Girl Collection

## Positioning

Smutty Good Girl is a Charmed & Dark bookish capsule collection for dark-romance and romantasy readers. It is intentionally merchandised as a collection rather than as miscellaneous novelty products.

## Launch placement

- Permanent discovery under the Shop menu
- Dedicated collection page at `/collections/smutty-good-girl`
- Featured on the New Drops page
- Featured on the homepage below the core Bags, Candles, and Dark Home sections
- Dedicated section/filter inside the main Shop experience

## Launch products

- Smutty Good Girl Society Tote
- Smutty Good Girl Reading Fuel Accent Coffee Mug (15oz)
- S. G. G. Enchanted Reads Water Bottle, 20oz
- S.G.G. Secret Society Water Bottle, 20oz

## Brand guardrails

- Do not call the line “novelty” in customer-facing copy.
- Keep Kiss Lock Bags, Candles, and Dark Home above S.G.G. in homepage hierarchy.
- Use dark-romance, bookish, blush, black, burgundy, candlelight, and reading-nook imagery.
- Keep S.G.G. out of the Kiss Lock Bags Google Shopping campaign.
